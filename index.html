Detection Flow

From detection to safety — in seconds.

01
CLIMAS Monitors Continuously
Gas, temperature and humidity are checked non-stop in the background. Every 5 seconds a reading is posted to the cloud — logged silently so you never have to think about it.
02
Data Analysed in Real Time
The device compares each reading against the configured safety threshold. Normal readings are logged to your cloud dashboard. Unusual changes are flagged immediately.
03
Dangerous Level Detected
When gas concentration exceeds the safety threshold, the device immediately triggers a loud local alarm. This happens within milliseconds regardless of internet connection.
04
Cloud Alert Sent to Your Phone
Simultaneously, a push notification is sent to your phone and the danger status appears on your dashboard. You are notified whether you are in the next room or on the other side of the world.
05
Emergency Call Placed (Pro)
If gas levels stay dangerous for 8 seconds, CLIMAS places an automated voice call to your emergency contact. The call alerts them that gas has been detected at your home. Pro plan only.

About CLIMAS

Built to protect
the homes we love.

CLIMAS was created to solve a common but dangerous problem — gas leaks happen without warning, and most homes have no way to detect them until it is too late.

Our Story

Gas is used in millions of homes across Ghana every day for cooking and heating. It is affordable, convenient and powerful. But it is also invisible and odourless in dangerous concentrations — making leaks extremely hard to detect without the right tools.

CLIMAS was built from a simple belief: every home deserves real-time awareness of what is in the air. We combined affordable IoT hardware with a cloud platform to create a safety system that is always on, always watching and always ready to alert you.

We started with the kitchen — the most common place for gas incidents — and designed CLIMAS to sit quietly in the background, monitoring continuously without interrupting daily life. When something is wrong, it does not wait. It acts immediately.
